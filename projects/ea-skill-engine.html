EA AI Skill Engine

Autonomous capability discovery for the modern enterprise.

Overview

The EA AI Skill Engine is a specialized tool developed to automate the heavy lifting of Enterprise Architecture discovery. By leveraging AI to scan organizational tech stacks (ERP, CRM, BI), it maps disparate software tools directly to business capabilities, aligning them with TOGAF standards.


The Problem

Traditional EA discovery is slow, manual, and often outdated by the time it's finished. Manufacturing giants and complex enterprises struggle to maintain a real-time view of their application landscape.


The Solution

Using autonomous search and synthesis engines, the tool creates a live "capability-first" map. It identifies core systems like SAP S/4HANA or LeadSquared and documents their role in the enterprise value chain automatically.
