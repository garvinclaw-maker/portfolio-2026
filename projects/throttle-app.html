ThrottleApp.

Enhancing rider safety and connectivity through a biker-centric tech ecosystem.

Overview

ThrottleApp is a conceptual mobile application designed as a comprehensive companion for motorcyclists. It solves the fragmentation in rider-tech by combining navigation, social connectivity, and safety into a single intuitive interface.

Key Features

  • Crash Detection: Utilizing smartphone accelerometers and gyroscopes to detect incidents and alert emergency contacts.
  • Live Location Sharing: Real-time tracking for group rides to ensure no rider is left behind.
  • Automated Trip Logging: Seamlessly documenting route data, speed, and photos without manual input.

Product Vision

The project focused on identifying core rider pain points—such as safety during solo rides and the difficulty of managing group dynamics—and solving them through an MVP that prioritizes performance and battery efficiency on the road.

Role: Product Lead / Lead Designer
