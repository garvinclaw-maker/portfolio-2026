Product Strategy

Smartwatch Landscape.

Strategic evolution mapping of the global wearables market.

Context

As the wearables market matures, the transition from simple fitness trackers to medical-grade health monitoring devices presents significant product strategy challenges. This project maps that evolution.

Strategic Analysis

I conducted a deep-dive into the current smartwatch landscape, analyzing market leaders (Apple, Samsung, Garmin) vs. emerging challengers. The focus was on identifying the "Blue Ocean" opportunities in localized health monitoring and AI-driven predictive wellness.

Key Insights

  • Ecosystem Locking: How software interoperability has replaced hardware specs as the primary driver for retention.
  • Battery as a UX Constraint: Analyzing the trade-offs between computational power and user experience.
  • The Future of Sensors: Mapping the roadmap for non-invasive glucose monitoring and metabolic tracking.

Deliverable: Market Research & Product Roadmap

Focus: Product Strategy, Competitor Analysis
