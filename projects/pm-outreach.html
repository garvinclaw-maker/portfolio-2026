PM Outreach Engine.

Scaling professional networking using autonomous agentic workflows.

The Challenge

Transitioning into high-impact Product Management roles (18+ LPA target) requires high-volume, high-quality networking. Manually identifying target companies, finding decision-makers, and crafting personalized outreach is a full-time job.

The Solution

I built an autonomous "Outreach Orchestrator" using **OpenClaw**. The engine uses LLMs to:

  • Scan LinkedIn and job boards for roles matching specific criteria in West Pune.
  • Research the company's recent product launches or news.
  • Draft highly personalized networking messages that focus on adding value to the PM team.
  • Track status across a central Kanban board for real-time monitoring.

Outcome

This project demonstrates the application of Enterprise Architecture thinking to personal growth—treating career transition as a scalable business process.

Stack: Python, OpenClaw, Brave Search API, iMessage integration
