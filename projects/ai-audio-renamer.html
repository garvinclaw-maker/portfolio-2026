AI Audio Renamer

Intelligent semantic file organization for recording artists.

Overview

Designed for the creative chaos of music recording, this AI-powered utility solves the problem of generic audio file naming (e.g., "Audio_Track_01.wav"). It uses Large Language Models to generate unique, theme-specific names based on the artist's creative direction.


The Creative Edge

Instead of manual renaming, the artist shares a "theme" or "mood" (e.g., "Cyberpunk", "Oceanic Drift"). The engine then bulk-renames all session files with evocative, non-repeating words that match that vibe. This makes searching through takes much more intuitive and keeps the creative momentum high.


Impact

Streamlines the post-recording workflow for independent artists, allowing them to spend less time on file management and more time on the mix.
